Our Story

No matter where you travel in Europe (whether on land or on a cruise), it’s good to be prepared for your trip, but do not book all in advance, maybe the first 1-2 nights. From there on try to stay spontaneous and flexible and see what happens. For many destinations that works, unless you travel to the very popular places, which are usually the big cities. There you should book your room far in advance. Get lost when in you are in a new destination and wander around. Walk more than usual. You will then have experiences which you will never forget… and to 99% in a positive way.
